About the Rhode Island Lottery

Located at 1425 Pontiac Avenue in Cranston, the Rhode Island Lottery oversees all aspects of Lottery operations and gambling in Rhode Island.

The mission of the Rhode Island Lottery is to generate revenue for the state by offering fun and innovative gambling products while maintaining a commitment to responsible play.

The revenue the Lottery generates for the state benefits all who live, work and visit the Ocean State.
