How To Play Daily Keno

  • Select play amount of $1, $2, $3, $4, $5, $10 or $20.
  • Select numbers between 1 and 80 based on the spot chosen, at least 1 number but not more than 10, using a playslip or let the system Quick Pick them for you.

    ex. If you select the 4 spot you must select 4 numbers.

PURCHASE DAILY KENO FOR MULTIPLE DRAWINGS
You can buy a ticket for up to twenty-five consecutive drawings.

HOW TO PLAY DAILY KENO FOR FUTURE DRAWINGS
Daily Keno tickets can be purchased for future dates. This is called Advance Play. The future draw date desired must be between the current draw date (the draw that is next to happen based on the day the ticket is purchased) and the twenty-fifth consecutive draw date.

Drawings are conducted every night at 8 p.m. Pacific Time. Ticket sales are cut-off every night at 7:45 p.m. and resume at 7:46 p.m.

For more detailed how-to-play information, contact the Lottery at 1-800-545-7510, Monday-Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Pacific Time (except state holidays). TDD/TTY: (360) 810-2849.

You must be 18 years of age or older to purchase Washington's Lottery tickets.
